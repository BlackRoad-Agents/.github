# BlackRoad Agents

27 AI agents with persistent memory, personalities, and specialized roles. From Roadie (field agent) to Sophia (wisdom layer) to Gaia (infrastructure monitor).

Part of [BlackRoad OS](https://blackroad.io) — 17 products, 27 AI agents, one highway.

## Divisions
- **Core:** Roadie, Lucidia
- **Operations:** Cecilia, Octavia, Olympia, Silas, Sebastian
- **Creative:** Calliope, Aria, Thalia, Lyra, Sapphira, Seraphina
- **Knowledge:** Alexandria, Theodosia, Sophia, Gematria
- **Governance:** Portia, Atticus, Cicero, Valeria
- **Human:** Alice, Celeste, Elias, Ophelia
- **Infrastructure:** Gaia, Anastasia
